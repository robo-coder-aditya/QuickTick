Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I get started with QuickTick?

A: Once you log in, you'll see your dashboard with an overview of your tasks. You can start by adding new tasks or checking your current and upcoming ones.

Q: How do I add a new task?

A: Click on the “+ Add new task” button, typically located in the top right section of the dashboard. This will open a form where you can enter the details for your new task.

Q: Can I edit the details of an existing task?

A: Yes, next to each task description (e.g., “Team meeting”), there is an edit icon (a pencil). Clicking this icon should allow you to modify the task’s details.

Q: What do the “Today,” “This Week”, and "This Month” tabs do?

A: These tabs allow you to filter your task view. “Today” shows tasks due or current for the present day, “This Week” displays tasks for the current week, and "This Month” shows tasks scheduled for the current month.
